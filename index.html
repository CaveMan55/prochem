Welcome to ProChem

Residential and commercial property owners are choosing Pro-Chem as the service to rid their homes and buildings of pests such as termites, bedbugs, rodents, flies, etc. Our team of trained experts has the latest equipment and techniques to make sure that pests are eliminated from your property with efficiency and high efficacy. We deal with more than the pests mentioned above and have the experience and skill to ensure that your home and family are always protected from infestation of dangerous insects and rodents.

What makes Pro-Chem unique from other pest control services in the area? Well, for one, we have the experience to make sure that all projects are taken care of with professionalism and without causing any inconveniences to the owners of the property. Our team of trained exterminators works from top to bottom to rid your home of the nuisances that can cause health problems and destroy your home.

We take on both interior and exterior extermination projects and you can hire us on a quarterly or monthly basis. We leave that decision up to you once you see for yourself the excellent services that we provide. Having pests infest your property can lead to many problems and your expenses can rocket sky-high if you do not take preventive measures at the early stage.

Pro-Chem can provide you with the most affordable yet excellent and professional extermination services in the industry. Our team of dedicated experts is noted for their skill and dedication to making your home or commercial property a safe and clean space that is free of pests. Our goal is to make sure that you are always given high-quality service and we also ensure that products that are safe for you and your family are always used while ridding your property of rodents, termites, cockroaches, bedbugs and flies, to name just a few. We also provide moisture control services.

By allowing Pro-Chem to take care of all your requirements for pest control, you guarantee yourself and your family a safe place that is free of unwanted pests. We take care of every inch of your home as well as the outdoors to make sure that every nook and cranny is pest-free!
